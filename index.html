Must Play
Elden Ring Resident Evil 4 Remake Ghostwire

Elden Ring is an ARPG set in a fantasy where players can create their own character and explore an open-world game with bosses. the game features a similar play style to the previous FromSoftware game Dark Souls. The game has received acclaim and was awarded Game Of The Year.

RE4 is a remake of the 2005 Resident Evil 4 game that follows Leon Kennedy from the series' previous games as he is enlisted to rescue the president's daughter that has been kidnapped. The remake has included new graphics, DLCs, and updated gameplay mechanics making is a worthwhile play even if you have not played previous installments.

Ghostwire: Tokyo is an Action game where you play as Akito a man who is spared from derath right before tokyo is turned into a ghost towned and overrun by spirits. Throughout the game you fight spirits in order to save the souls of those vanished. The game is slowpaced and does take a while to understand dealing with themes of urban isolation.
